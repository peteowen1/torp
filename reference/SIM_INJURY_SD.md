# Per-team per-round injury/disruption noise SD (points) Represents week-to-week variation in effective team strength due to player availability, minor injuries, and roster changes.

Per-team per-round injury/disruption noise SD (points) Represents
week-to-week variation in effective team strength due to player
availability, minor injuries, and roster changes.

## Usage

``` r
SIM_INJURY_SD
```

## Format

An object of class `numeric` of length 1.
