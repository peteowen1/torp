# Reduced injury noise SD when known injuries are excluded (points) When specific injured players are removed from team ratings, the remaining per-round noise can be smaller since a major source of variation is gone.

Reduced injury noise SD when known injuries are excluded (points) When
specific injured players are removed from team ratings, the remaining
per-round noise can be smaller since a major source of variation is
gone.

## Usage

``` r
SIM_INJURY_SD_KNOWN
```

## Format

An object of class `numeric` of length 1.
