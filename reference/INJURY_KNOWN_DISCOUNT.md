# Injury discount for teams with known injuries excluded Lighter than SIM_INJURY_DISCOUNT (0.95) because major absences are already reflected by removing those players from the rating sum.

Injury discount for teams with known injuries excluded Lighter than
SIM_INJURY_DISCOUNT (0.95) because major absences are already reflected
by removing those players from the rating sum.

## Usage

``` r
INJURY_KNOWN_DISCOUNT
```

## Format

An object of class `numeric` of length 1.
