# Scaling factor for familiarity-based Grand Final advantage (points). Multiplied by familiarity difference (home - away) to produce a venue-specific GF advantage. E.g., a Melbourne MCG tenant with 0.50 familiarity vs an interstate team with 0.05 gives a diff of 0.45, yielding 0.45 \* 6 = ~2.7 points advantage.

Scaling factor for familiarity-based Grand Final advantage (points).
Multiplied by familiarity difference (home - away) to produce a
venue-specific GF advantage. E.g., a Melbourne MCG tenant with 0.50
familiarity vs an interstate team with 0.05 gives a diff of 0.45,
yielding 0.45 \* 6 = ~2.7 points advantage.

## Usage

``` r
SIM_GF_FAMILIARITY_SCALE
```

## Format

An object of class `numeric` of length 1.
